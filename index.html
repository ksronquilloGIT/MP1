A Simple Idea to a Thriving Company


RBA Graphics Sign Advertising Corp. is offering great customer service at competitive prices. We started with the founder’s interest in graphics and prints and the idea of gift-giving. With these passions, it eventually ventured into personalized gifts on a small scale. High-volume orders and more demands had finally given us the opportunity to officially start small but grow steadily in expertise and experience.
Fotoshow was launched and registered in 2016. At first, we offered a variety of digital prints and personalization products and services. Then eventually we shifted our main focus to large-format printing and signage. And now, Fotoshow offers only the highest quality yet inexpensive services in the areas of prints, signage, and giveaways.
Our close-knit and professional team of administration, staff, and personnel provides a full customer service that keeps our customers coming back to us for their needs. Our satisfied clients are our greatest help in our marketing campaign. And their feedback is our greatest gift to better improve our services. As a testament, we have now been in service for more than 5 years.
We value our clients. And with that in mind, we don’t just meet customer satisfaction; we also do our best to exceed it. There is nothing too big or too small for us; every client is unique and valuable. We exert all our efforts and creativity to provide customized services to our clients.

Our Commitment


Affordability with quality and good customer service.

Our Vision


To be one of the best one-stop shops for prints, signage, and giveaways that are cost-effective without compromising on quality. We aim to be first in the minds of our customers by providing uncompromised personalized service.

Our Mission


To do our business as an expression of worship to God through providing quality and affordable prints, signage, and giveaways, we continuously improve to exceed our customers' expectations. Our commitment to our customers' satisfaction translates into a relationship that's mutually lasting and beneficial.
